&raquo; Germany, officially the Federal Republic 
of Germany,is a federal parliamentary republic 
in western-central Europe. <br/><br/
&raquo;The country consists 
 of 16 states and its capital and largest city is Berlin.
<br/><br/>
&raquo;Germany has the largest population of any EU country.<br/><br/>
&raquo;The English word Germany derives from the Latin Germania,
which came into use after Julius Caesar adopted it
for the peoples east of the Rhine.<br/><br/>
&raquo;The German term Deutschland 
(originally diutisciu land, "the German lands") is 
derived from deutsch, descended from Old High German diutisc
"popular" (i.e. belonging to the diot or diota "people"), 
originally used to distinguish the language of the common 
people from Latin and its Romance descendants.<br/><br/>
